Basic - Branding Guideline
This document establishes the branding principles for Mosaic Trading S.C.’s subsidiary, Mosaic-Furi General Hospital, providing a
clear framework to ensure all communications consistently reﬂect our identity.
The purpose of these guidelines is to create a cohesive, professional, and recognizable brand presence across all touchpoints –
from printed materials and signage to digital and social media platforms. Consistent application of these principles strengthens
brand recognition, builds trust with audience, and reinforces the values of quality, care, and innovation that deﬁne Mosaic-Furi
General Hospital.
This guide serves as a practical reference for anyone producing branded materials, ensuring that every communication is aligned,
engaging, and on-brand. Adherence to these standards is essential to maintaining a uniﬁed visual and verbal identity that
represents the organization’s mission and vision with clarity and professionalism.

What not to do
Do not alter the logo artwork in any way, and only use it in the colors speciﬁed in these guidelines. This means no changes or tweaks
to color, proportions, or typeface. Do not rearrange any elements within the logo. Avoid placing the logo on top of low-contrast or
busy backgrounds.
Always use the high-resolution, original logo ﬁles supplied in the asset pack. Do not screenshot and paste the logo, to avoid
degrading it in quality.

Co-branding
The placement of partner logos alongside the Global Partnership logo should respect the exclusion zone rules. Individual logos
should be sized to a similar height and vertically centered. This guidance accommodates a range of differently sized logos and
maintains a visual balance to the set of logos as a whole.
